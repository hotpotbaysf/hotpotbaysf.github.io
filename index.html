About Us

A modern hotpot destination offering a wide variety of rich, flavorful soup bases and an exceptional selection of premium Wagyu beef as the star of the experience. We specialize in beautifully marbled, high-quality Wagyu cuts that deliver unmatched tenderness and depth of flavor. Enjoy fresh ingredients, customizable house-made sauces, and a warm, stylish dining atmosphere—perfect for groups, celebrations, and true food lovers seeking an elevated hotpot experience.

Whether it's a casual meal or a special gathering, we invite you to enjoy hotpot the way it should be.
